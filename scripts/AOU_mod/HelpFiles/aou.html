About AO Universe What is AO Universe? A fansite run by players, for players. It all began as a project back in September of 2005 and with a bit of luck and strong support from the AO community we haven't stopped since. What the site provides (links will open http://ao-universe.com in your internet browser): Social & RP content: This includes stories from players, revisiting past events which shaped AO's storyline, past and current details on official storyline content, a database with background stories of characters (of registered users), RP related news from the roleplaying community as well as the "Rubi-Ka Times" section. We also provide an Events Calendar for everyone to advertise any type of event. Knowledge content: Thanks to everyone who has contributed over the years! Every guide credits players who provided the info; if you have a gameplay guide to contribute please use our contact page! We currently have hundreds of guides that covers Classic AO, Shadowlands, Alien Invasion, Lost Eden and Booster Packs. We are the largest AO knowledge source worldwide! PvP content: This includes the latest activity of Notum Wars battles across all servers, stats, listing of organizations involved and the exact time of each event. Additional content/download: We also host and link overall utilities used by the community, like ChartConverter, ScriptEditor, Guild Database, Server Status, Clicksaver, AOIA, AOShopAgent, AOScripter, Launcher Skins, Wallpapers, etc. We have a hundreds of screenshots, original player-made comics and art, images and AO concept art at our Gallery. Forums: We provide a set of forums for all types of players to interact, exchange information, share AO stories, and more. We support other player-run fansites like Gridstream Productions, AOFroobs, RecipeNET, Newland News, and any group of players or individual player who brings something to the game for others to enjoy. Those who want to become a Team members/volunteer do so on their own accord, no pay, only gratification will be... you did it for the community, the friends you play with, and the love for the game. Our Generous Sponsor: Currently we're hosted by IOS-Solutions at no cost, their contribution to the AO Community. Special thanks: To Khuri for hooking us up with our hosting and domain and to the many players who supported this project, you made this happen! To the wonderful AOU Team who have built the site from a player's point of view with their dedication and love for the game making AOU a high quality fansite. Special Note: The banners currently at our front page is to advertise guilds, other AO fansites who added an AOU banner to their site, in-character fictitious advertising, ARK and the Official Anarchy Online website. So check out http://ao-universe.com and tell your AO friends and guildmates about us!
